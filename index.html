Landslide Proposal

September 9th 2020 Overview The purpose of this project is to solve major landslide issues in developing countries. The major causes of landslides are slope, water runoff, and deforestation. Attempting to prevent a landslide with retaining walls and heavy machinery is too expensive in developing countries. Thus, the most cost effective solution will be to plant trees in these high risk areas. Goals
  • Create an app that will show dangerous landslide areas.
  • Start with one country: Columbia.
  • Show users where they can plant to prevent the landslides.
